Kopi Kenangan's Customer Reviews Dashboard

Dashboard · Jun 2023

Python, GPT-3.5, Tableau, Pandas

Problem Statement

A chain of restaurants, "Kopi Kenangan," operates multiple branches across different cities in Indonesia. As the business continues to grow, the management team is eager to understand the overall satisfaction level of their customers.

The objective of this analysis is to identify any patterns or trends in customer ratings that could impact the business. By uncovering insights from the data, Kopi Kenangan aims to make data-driven decisions to improve the customer experience and maintain high satisfaction levels across all branches.

Collection

Scraped Kopi Kenangan's Google Maps review data using Pandas across 5 branches, resulting in more than 1,500+ reviews.

Transformation

Utilized GPT-3.5 for extracting key information from review text, such as topic, sentiment, and tags (e.g. service, place, food). The extracted features were then visualized in an interactive Tableau dashboard comparing customer satisfaction across branches.
